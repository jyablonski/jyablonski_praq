# Car Insurance

Bodily Injury Liability - $15k / $30k - pays for damage for people injured or killed in an accident that you were responsible for. Also covers legal defense if you're sued.

Property Damage - $5k - pays for damage to other people's propery resulting from an accident caused by your vehicle that you're responsible for.

Uninsured & Underinsured Motorist - $15k / $30k -  pays for you and your passengers' bodily injury damages caused by an uninsured, underinsured, or hit-and-run accident involving contact with the uninsured or underinsured vehicle.

Comprehensive - pays for damage to your car from anything that's not an accident like theft, vandalism, flood, fire, animals etc.

Collision - $1k deductible - Pays for damage to your auto minus your selected deductible caused by collision with another object or when it overturns. You pay the deductible, they pay the rest.

Uninsured Motorist Property Damage

If you have a $1,000 collision deductible and you’re in an accident caused by an uninsured driver, without the waiver, you’d need to pay $1,000 out of pocket to get your car fixed. With the waiver, you wouldn’t have to pay anything—the insurance would cover the entire cost, as long as the other driver or their vehicle is identified.

## What to do after Accident


- Stay calm, park car at side of road
- Get out and exchange Insurance Info with driver
- Do not say sorry or admit fault
- Call Police if needed for things like 1 of you are injured, road cleanup, ambulance, or tow truck.
