# Business Platforms

General overview of various business platforms categorized by their primary functions.

## Data & Privacy Platforms

- CMP - Consent Management Platform

  - Purpose: Manages user consent for cookies and data processing to comply with privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
  - OneTrust, Cookiebot, TrustArc, Osano, Didomi

- CDP - Customer Data Platform

  - Purpose: Unifies customer data from multiple touchpoints into a single, real-time profile for personalized marketing and customer experiences.
  - Segment, Adobe Real-Time CDP, Salesforce Customer 360, Tealium, mParticle

- DMP - Data Management Platform

  - Purpose: Collects and organizes anonymous audience data (mainly third-party cookies) for targeted advertising and audience segmentation.
  - Adobe Audience Manager, Oracle BlueKai, Lotame, Krux (now Salesforce), The Trade Desk

- PIM - Privacy Information Management
  - Purpose: Manages comprehensive privacy compliance programs including data mapping, risk assessments, and privacy request automation.
  - OneTrust, TrustArc, DataGrail, Transcend, BigID

## Marketing & Advertising Platforms

- DSP - Demand-Side Platform

  - Purpose: Allows advertisers to automatically bid on and purchase digital ad inventory across multiple ad exchanges in real-time.
  - The Trade Desk, Google Display & Video 360, Amazon DSP, Adobe Advertising Cloud, MediaMath

- SSP - Supply-Side Platform

  - Purpose: Helps publishers sell their ad inventory by connecting to multiple ad exchanges and optimizing revenue from available ad space.
  - Google Ad Manager, Amazon Publisher Services, OpenX, PubMatic, Magnite

- DCP - Data Clean Platform

  - Purpose: Cleanses, standardizes, and enriches customer data to improve data quality and remove duplicates or errors.
  - Informatica, Talend, Trifacta, DataLadder, Validity (BriteVerify)

- MAP - Marketing Automation Platform

  - Purpose: Automates repetitive marketing tasks like email campaigns, lead scoring, and customer nurturing to scale personalized marketing efforts.
  - HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign

- CRM - Customer Relationship Management

  - Purpose: Manages customer interactions, sales pipelines, and relationship data to improve sales processes and customer service.
  - Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Pipedrive, Zoho

- ESP - Email Service Provider
  - Purpose: Delivers bulk email campaigns with features like templates, automation, analytics, and deliverability optimization.
  - Mailchimp, Constant Contact, SendGrid, Campaign Monitor, Klaviyo

## Analytics & Optimization

- WA - Web Analytics

  - Purpose: Tracks and analyzes website visitor behavior to understand user engagement, conversion paths, and site performance.
  - Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Hotjar

- A/B Testing Platforms

  - Purpose: Tests different versions of web pages, emails, or app features to determine which performs better for specific goals.
  - Optimizely, VWO, Google Optimize, Split.io, Unbounce

- APM - Application Performance Monitoring

  - Purpose: Monitors application performance, uptime, and user experience to identify and resolve technical issues before they impact users.
  - New Relic, Datadog, AppDynamics, Dynatrace, Splunk

- BI - Business Intelligence Platform
  - Purpose: Transforms raw business data into actionable insights through dashboards, reports, and data visualization tools.
  - Tableau, Power BI, Looker, Qlik Sense, Sisense

## Customer Experience

- CX - Customer Experience Platform

  - Purpose: Manages all customer touchpoints and support interactions to deliver consistent, high-quality customer service across channels.
  - Zendesk, Freshworks, ServiceNow, Qualtrics, Adobe Experience Cloud

- CMS - Content Management System

  - Purpose: Enables non-technical users to create, edit, and manage website content without requiring coding knowledge.
  - WordPress, Drupal, Contentful, Webflow, Squarespace

- DAM - Digital Asset Management
  - Purpose: Centralizes storage, organization, and distribution of digital assets like images, videos, and brand materials for easy access and consistency.
  - Bynder, Widen, Canto, Adobe Experience Manager Assets, Brandfolder
